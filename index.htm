What is My Toodoo?

My Toodoo is a simple web-app that helps you to manage your to-do tasks.

How to use My Toodoo?

  • To add a task, type your new task into the above white box.
  • To remove a task, click on the button which is next to it.
  • To edit a task, click on the content of that task, then edit it on the above white box.
  • To remove all of the tasks, click on the button Reset right below the white box.

Features

  • Add a task.
  • Remove a task.
  • Edit a task.
  • Store the list of tasks into the Local Storage.
  • Reset the Local Storage (remove the whole tasks list).
  • Responsive design.
